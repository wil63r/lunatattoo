About Us

Walking down Empedrado Street, a few meters from the emblematic Bodeguita del Medio in the heart of Old Havana in Cuba, it is enough to have a look inside a door and see the stairs painted with bright colors that leads to our tattoo shop.

With more than three years of experience, in Luna Tattoo, we are determined to look for and create that design you want, even without knowing what it is. We have no limit in terms of design, whether portraits, tribal, Asian, biomechanical, color or gray and black.

Tattoo is a passion for us. In every tattoo we make, regardless of the design or size, we put all our efforts with the aim of making the client feel proud of it.

Services We Offer

Tattoo is a permanent, custom and handmade piece of art.

Unique Designs

We have an extensive catalog of designs, but, if you do not find what you want, we design one according to the characteristics you want it to have.

Fixes

Those marks in your body or that old tattoos that you think will disfigure your body are not a problem for us. Our experience allows us to carry out a work on these marks or tattoos that will later feel proud to wear and show.

Retouch

If your tattoo has lost its colors and is no longer the same as when it was made, we have inks of the best quality which, when retouched, will look like never before.
